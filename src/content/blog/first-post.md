My mother got kicked out of Canada, like the entire country, at 4 AM this morning. 

Ok, not really but I dreamed she did. It was during a craft festival of some kind. I think she was selling things as "handmade" hwhen they really weren't.  Some folks came up and accused her of being a fraud and she was actually TRESPASSED from the country of Canada. HAHA!! 

This dream was so confusing at first but after thinking about it more, I realized this was justice. She was finally being seen for what she really was. A fraud. Not just because of the bad checks she bounced, but her claims at being a mom. She wasn't a mom, she was a mother.  A mother who mistreated and neglected her daughter in the privacy of her home but was the person who helped everyone, was so nice, .. everyone loved her.  So when I tried to get help, to tell people what was going on.. nobody believed me. Not even my own grandmother. She actually seemed shocked and tried to shame me when I told her I hadn't been in contact with my mother since she left the state. She asked how she was doing and I told her I didn't know, nor did I care. Her response "She was always nice to me."  Yeah, that's the issue.

So.. seeing her kicked out of an entire country for being a fraud. Poetic justice!!
